The Restaurant

The Peking Restaurant in the heart of Bugibba is a lively place where the cooking is oriental and the vibe contemporary, with paisley walls, a glass pond, a water fountain, an aquarium and bright red touches creating a good oriental feel. This family owned restaurant has been a mainstay of its neighborhood for years.
The menu includes soups, starters, duck, seafood, beef, chicken, pork, vegetables, sizzling dishes and savories. Favorites ‘aromatic and crispy duck’, ‘sweet and sour pork’, ‘beef oyster’, ‘sizzling prawns’ and ‘curry chicken’. Very good wine list to compliment each dish.
We also offer free deliveries to surrounding areas.
